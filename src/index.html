SimpleCookies in a oven


1 tsp smooth butter
1.5 tsp brown sugar
5 tsp granulated sugar
0.5 tsp vanilla sugar
1 egg
5 tsp cake flour

Before you start, preheat the oven to 150°C.

Mix the brown sugar with the butter until you get a creamy base.
Add the granulated sugar and vanilla sugar, and mix well.
Gradually mix in the cake flour until everything is well combined.
Crack the egg into the mixture and stir until the egg is fully incorporated.
You now have the cookie dough.
Bake at 150°C for 15 minutes.
